INDIAN SIGN LANGUAGE

ISL to Alphabet

The Indian Sign Language to Alphabets model translates hand gestures into corresponding letters of the alphabet. This innovative tool enables efficient communication for the hearing-impaired, facilitating literacy and language learning by converting sign language gestures into written symbols, enhancing accessibility and inclusivity in educational and social settings.

ISL to WORD

The Indian Sign Language to Words model efficiently converts hand gestures into written text, bridging communication gaps for individuals with hearing impairments. By accurately translating sign language into words, it enhances accessibility and fosters inclusive interactions, empowering the deaf community to engage more fully in various contexts.


ISL to Hindi Language

The Text-to-Speech model converts written text into spoken words, utilizing advanced algorithms to generate natural-sounding speech. This technology enhances accessibility for the visually impaired, assists in language learning, and enables hands-free interaction with devices, empowering users to consume information and communicate more effectively in various contexts.
