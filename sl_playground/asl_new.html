AMERICAN SIGN LANGUAGE

Tutorial

The American Sign Language Tutorials model provides interactive lessons on ASL, guiding users through learning gestures, expressions, and grammar. Utilizing multimedia resources, it fosters proficiency in ASL communication, enhancing accessibility and inclusivity for the hearing-impaired community and facilitating cultural understanding and linguistic fluency.


Text to Speech

The Text-to-Speech in Multiple Languages model converts written text into spoken words across various languages. With support for diverse linguistic contexts, it enables seamless communication and accessibility for multilingual users. This versatile tool enhances language learning, facilitates international communication, and promotes inclusivity in diverse cultural settings.

ASL to Word

The American Sign Language to Words Conversion model translates ASL gestures into written text. Using advanced recognition algorithms, it accurately converts sign language expressions into words, facilitating communication accessibility for the hearing-impaired. This technology bridges language barriers and promotes inclusivity in various educational, social, and professional settings.
